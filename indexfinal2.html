The Taj Mahal of Agra

The Taj Mahal of Agra is widely considered one of the most beautiful buildings ever created.The Taj Mahal is the Jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the World's Heritage.

Goa Beach Beauty

Goa is one of the most favorite destination among Indian tourists due to its pristine beaches.Dotted with hundreds of impressive beaches in Goa,

Must Visit Forts

Forts are one of the richest monuments in India built by the Great kings thousands of years ago for the defence of their kingdom.

THE SUBTITLE

Amber Fort, Rajasthan

Amber Fort is a Jewel of Jaipur, with its majestic, beautiful architecture and rich history. Built by the legendary Raja Man Singh sometime in the 17th century,
the fort-cum-palace has held strong despite the march of invading dynasties through the centuries

THE SUBTITLE

Pratapgad Fort

Pratapgad Fort is a huge mountainous fort built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj situated in the Satara District of Maharashtra state
It is very important fort as the major Battle with AfzalKhan took place here on 10th November 1659.
The height of this fort is 3543 feet above sea level.

THE SUBTITLE

The Red Fort

The Red Fort is located in the city of Delhi in India.It was built by Mughal Emperor ShahJahan as the palace fort of his capital Shahjahanabad.
It has a great history as it was the main residence of Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years (until 1857).

THE SUBTITLE

Murud-Janjira

It is India's strongest marine fort. Recent records show that Murud-Janjira was built at the end of the
17th century and has seen some historical figures. The fort remained unconquered till it became part of the Indian Territory in 1947.

THE SUBTITLE

The Gwalior Fort

Gwalior Fort, situated on top of a hill, finds its place among the best fortresses of India. It is also considered to be one of the most impenetrable forts in the country.
Known for its great architecture and rich past, Gwalior Fort is a must-visit attraction

THE SUBTITLE

Chittorgarh Fort

The Chittorgarh Fort is one of the largest forts in India. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
It is famous for the Jauhar that Rani Padmini and Rani Karnavati underwent during siege

Marine Drive, Mumbai

Marine Drive is popularly known as Queens Necklace because,
when viewed at night from an elevated point anywhere along the drive, the street lights resemble a string of pearls in a necklace.

The INDIA GATE

India Gate, official name Delhi Memorial, originally called All-India War Memorial, monumental sandstone arch in New Delhi,
dedicated to the troops of British India who died in wars fought between 1914 and 1919.

Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Andaman and Nicobar Islands is a union territory of India consisting of 572 islands, of which 38 are inhabited, at the junction of the Bay of Bengal and the Andaman Sea.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus is a historic terminal train station and UNESCO World Heritage Site in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

Train to Shimla

Kalka Shimla Railway (KSR), a 96.6 kilometer long a single track working rail link was built in the mid-19th century to provide a service to the highland town of Shimla,
it is emblematic of the technical and material efforts to disincline mountain populations through the railway.
